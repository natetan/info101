Problems with Registration

Specifically, there's one problem we're dealing with

Solution: Make a waitlist

Problems

  • Students have to fight to the death for classes
  • It's not fair to wake up everyday to fight the same fight
  • If you don't make it, you're stuck on the same level as everyone else, even if you have an earlier date

Solution

  • Have a waitlist, so you don't have to keep logging in to register
  • If someone gets out, the next person is put in
  • Think of it like a queue: first in, first out
  • No fight to the death
  • More sleep time for students
